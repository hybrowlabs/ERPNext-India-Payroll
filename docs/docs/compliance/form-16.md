# Form 16 & Annual Statement

**Form 16** is the employer's TDS certificate — a statutory document issued to every employee whose TDS was deducted during the financial year.

---

## Annual TDS Statement (internal)

Before generating Form 16, review the **Annual TDS Statement** per employee. This is accessible from any submitted salary slip — click **Annual Statement**.

The statement shows:
- Month-wise earnings broken down by component.
- Month-wise deductions (EPF, PT, ESIC, TDS).
- HRA exemption breakup (if Old Regime and rent > 0).
- LTA reimbursement details.
- Chapter VIA deductions from the approved declaration.
- Net taxable income, tax slab computation, surcharge, cess, rebate 87A.
- Total TDS deducted through the year.

---

## What Form 16 contains

**Part A** — TDS certificate (generated from TRACES, not from the app):
- Employer TAN, employee PAN.
- Quarter-wise TDS deposited.

**Part B** — Salary computation (generated by Indian Payroll):
- Gross salary (aggregate of all components).
- Exemptions claimed (HRA, LTA, etc.).
- Deductions under Chapter VIA.
- Taxable income.
- Tax computation (slab-wise).
- TDS deducted.

Indian Payroll generates the **Part B** portion as a structured PDF.

---

## Generating the annual statement PDF

1. Open any submitted salary slip for the employee (any month in the FY).
2. Click **Generate Annual Statement**.
3. The system fetches all salary slips for that employee in the payroll period, fetches the approved declaration, and renders the annual statement HTML template.
4. The PDF is returned inline for download.

---

## Form 16 DocType

Go to **HR → Payroll → Form 16** for a record per employee per financial year:
- Links to all salary slips.
- Stores the computed Part B data.
- Tracks issuance status.

---

## Timeline

| Deadline | Action |
|---|---|
| May 15 | Employer deposits Q4 TDS with TRACES |
| May 31 | Download Part A from TRACES |
| June 15 | Issue Form 16 (Part A + Part B) to employees |

:::info
Part A must be downloaded from TRACES and manually merged with the Part B PDF generated by Indian Payroll before issuing to employees.
:::
