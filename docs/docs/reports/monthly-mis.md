# Monthly Salary MIS

The **Monthly Salary MIS** (Management Information System) shows payroll totals aggregated by month — one row per month, not per employee. Designed for CFO/management dashboards and period-over-period trend analysis.

---

## Columns

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| Month | Calendar month name |
| Payroll Period | Financial year |
| No. of Employees | Head count with a salary slip in that month |
| CTC P.A. | Sum of all employees' Annual CTC |
| CTC P.M. | CTC P.A. / 12 |
| Gross Pay | Statutory gross pay total |
| Total Income | Sum of taxable income |
| Total Deduction | Sum of all deductions |
| Total Net Pay | Aggregate take-home |

---

## Filters

Company, Month, Payroll Period.

---

## Salary Reconciliation

For a month-over-month **employee-level** comparison (who got a raise? who is new? who exited?), use the **[Salary Reconciliation](./salary-reco)** report instead.
